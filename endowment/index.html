The Waqf Principle

The Sacred Civilization Research Archive does not operate on a commercial model. It does not sell manuscripts, seek institutional grants, or submit to peer review by any epistemic authority it has not itself recognised as legitimate.

The archive is sustained by the Waqf — the Islamic charitable endowment — in the tradition of the great civilizational libraries: Gondishapur, Bayt al-Hikma before its capture, and the dargah library networks of the Indus Basin. The Waqf is not a funding model. It is an act of Amanat — sacred trust — toward the future.
